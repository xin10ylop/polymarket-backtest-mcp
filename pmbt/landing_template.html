The trap →

A strategy can win 75.2% of the time and still lose money on every trade.

Buying dips at 40¢ with a 5¢ take-profit caps your wins at a nickel, but a forced resolution loses the full 40¢. The math is brutally asymmetric: break-even needs an 88.9% win rate. This tool exists to expose exactly that kind of trap before real money does.

What it does
[ engine ]

Composable strategy engine

Combine entry conditions (price level, price momentum, time remaining, BTC move) with limit or market execution and take-profit, stop-loss or timed exits. Described in plain English to Claude, executed deterministically by the server.

[ microstructure ]

Real market microstructure

Maker fills at your limit price, taker fills with slippage at the observed quote, a maker-free fee model, and ties resolve Up per official Polymarket rules.

[ walk-forward ]

Walk-forward ready

Every backtest accepts date ranges, so you can optimize in-sample and validate out-of-sample without rewriting a thing.
