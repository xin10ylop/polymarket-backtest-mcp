Polymarket BTC Backtester

A public MCP server for backtesting resting-limit strategies on Polymarket's "BTC Up or Down" 5-minute markets, built on 100ms historical quote data.

Data coverage: 2026-03-05 to 2026-04-25 UTC · 14,361 tradeable markets · 52 days

Add it to Claude

  1. Open Claude and go to Settings → Connectors
  2. Click Add custom connector
  3. Paste the URL above and save (no login or API key needed)
  4. In any chat, open the + menu and enable the connector

What it does

  • backtest — simulate strategies one trade per market across ~14,000 historical 5-minute markets. Reports win rate, expectancy, drawdown, equity curve, entry fill stats, and the exact exit breakdown (take-profit / stop / time exits vs forced $1/$0 resolutions).
  • composable strategies — build entries and exits from tested bricks: price levels, trailing price moves, time-to-close, BTC moves; limit (maker) or market (taker) entries; take-profit, stop-loss, time exits. Discover the schema with strategy_vocabulary.
  • data_coverage — the exact date range and any gap days behind every result.
  • list_markets / market_context / get_price_series — browse individual markets, winners, price-to-beat, and bid/ask history.

Maker fills (limit entries, take-profits) execute at the limit price, fee 0; taker fills (market entries, stops, time exits) execute at the observed quote, so gaps work against you. Resolution uses the official market outcomes, never exchange reference prices. Static historical dataset; nothing here is financial advice.

"Backtest buying Up at 40¢ with a 5¢ take-profit across all markets and show me the win rate, the skew breakdown, and the date range tested"
"Backtest buying Down at market when it's under 30¢ with under 90 seconds left, held to resolution"
